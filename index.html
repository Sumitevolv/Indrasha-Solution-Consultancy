How Can We Help?

We use a wide range of modern technologies tailored to each project's needs. For embedded systems: Embedded C, C++, Python, RTOS, ESP32, STM32, Arduino. For web development: React.js, Next.js, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, PostgreSQL. For UI/UX: Figma, Adobe XD, Framer.

We offer flexible pricing models to suit different needs: Project-based pricing starting at ₹5,000, hourly rates at ₹500/hour, or monthly retainers for ongoing work. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your project requirements.

Project timelines vary based on complexity: Basic websites (1-2 weeks), Complex web applications (3-8 weeks), Embedded systems prototypes (2-4 weeks), Production-ready hardware (6-12 weeks). We provide timeline estimates after project scoping.
